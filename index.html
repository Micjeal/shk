About Skahhe

Skahhe Travel Care (U) Ltd

Skahhe Travel Care (U) Ltd is an indigenous transport company in Uganda. The company has been in the transportation business for over 7 years.

SKAHHE understands the importance of having a clean, safe, well maintained and functional vehicle. The company has an extensive fleet of vehicles and able to meet different needs of our clientele.

The team of drivers are professional and reliable with experience driving around Kampala as well as upcountry. Skahhe travel care provides services for different organizations including international NGOs, local NGOs and government institutions.

The company specializes in provision of luxury air conditioned Vans (Drones/super custom), 4x4 vehicles (station wagons), Double cabins pickup, Coasters and saloon cars. The company cars are fully comprehensively insured and installed with GPS vehicle tracking device.

SKAHHE is open to doing business with you by giving you safe travels and quality services. The company team is available for a more detailed interaction and would be privileged to have you/organization as one of our valued clientele.
